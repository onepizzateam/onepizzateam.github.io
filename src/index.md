### About Me

I'm a student at BITS Pilani interested in product, AI, startups, and developer tools.

Currently working as a Product & AI Intern at _Volvo Group_, where I've been building machine intelligence and analytics systems for the Construction Equipment business. My work has included lifecycle analytics covering more than 27,000 sales events, forecasting and stall-risk prediction systems, and internal platforms that significantly reduced operational reporting time.

Previously, I led Partnerships & Alliances at _Conquest, BITS Pilani's startup accelerator._ Over two years, I closed more than _INR 90 lakh_ in sponsorship revenue, helped structure ecosystem partnerships, and contributed to the evaluation process for over 3,000 startup applications. I've also led product development from _0 to 1_ through Saathi AI and contributed to _open-source_ projects spanning infrastructure and _developer tooling._

Located between Bengaluru and Pilani 🇮🇳.

Currently studying a Bachelor's Degree in Manufacturing Engineering at _BITS Pilani._
